About AquaSense

Passionate about the ocean, dedicated to your safety and enjoyment.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, AquaSense began as a small group of friends who shared a deep love for the ocean. We wanted to create a diving center that wasn't just about certifications, but about creating a community of ocean stewards.

Over the years, we've grown into a premier diving destination, but our core values remain the same: safety, conservation, and fun. We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to experience the magic of the underwater world.

Our team of PADI-certified instructors brings decades of combined experience to every dive. Whether you're taking your first breath underwater or you're a seasoned pro, we're here to make your experience unforgettable.
